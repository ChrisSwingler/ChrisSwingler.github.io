The Origins of Baltimore Travels

How it all started

August 2011

On holiday in Baltimore, Ireland

A beautiful coastal setting for a summer holiday, I started to have the idea of writing stories for my daughter Eleanor who was yet to be born! 

The idea of writing stories for Eleanor based on my own travels emerged and to do this with a cuddly teddy being the main character. I thought he could pack the sunshine in his suitcase and so always have the sunshine and optimism with him wherever he travelled to. The title Sunshine in your Suitcase was born.                     

November 2017

Writing started on the first album - Sunshine in your Suitcase

When I finally got round to writing the stories (6 years later!) Baltimore seemed to be the ideal name for the teddy bear in the stories as that is where we had been on holiday. 

I returned to Istanbul where I had lived as a child and wrote the first story for Sunshine in your Suitcase in the district of Kadikoy full of cafes and delightful walks along the Bosphorous and Sea of Marmara coastline.

November 2018 - January 2019

Writing of 2nd album - Pop-up Radio Station

For the 2nd album, it emerged that Baltimore needed to have a role and be more than just a traveller having fun on travels around the world!

I thought he could become a Radio DJ and use his suitcase to carry a portable broadcasting unit so that he could broadcast a radio show from anywhere in any town!

November 2019

Writing completed on 3rd album - Lighthouse Hotel

Eleanor had the idea of having a hotel in a Lighthouse and I agreed to write a series of stories all about Baltimore on holiday at the Lighthouse hotel.
