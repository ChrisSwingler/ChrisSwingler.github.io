PHILIPPE RICORDEL

World Wildlife Photographer & Guide

Philippe takes people to places where they can take their own Wildlife photos under his guidance, getting to the right spots at the right time of year and day and with detailed advice on how to approach the wildlife without disturbing them. He only takes small groups, generally 4 or 5 people, so those that travel with Philippe get very personal attention and guidance throughout the Photographic Safari.

Philippe has detailed knowledge of the best places for Wildlife Photography in Brazil, Kenya, Rwanda, Norway, China, Japan, New Zealand and Antarctica.
