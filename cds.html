Baltimore Travels

DRIVE-TIME AUDIO ENTERTAINMENT FOR CHILDREN (6 - 9 YEARS)
3 Audio CDs - each with 6 short stories and musical interludes

Sunshine in your Suitcase - CD. 6 stories.

Istanbul - Zurich - Milan - Florence & African Safari 
Bonus: Baltimore and the Hats

Baltimore is from the seaside town of the same name in Ireland. He has always travelled since he was very young. We are going to follow Baltimore across the many miles that he travels and he will open our eyes to all sorts of wonders. Baltimore loves to travel the world. Wherever he goes, he opens his suitcase and out pops the sunshine.

Tracks
1. On the Bosphorous
Baltimore tries a grilled fish sandwich with fish caught fresh on the quayside and takes a ride on the ferries.
2. African Safari
Baltimore spots all sorts of animals with camera at the ready.
3. Swiss Chocolate
Baltimore tries ice skating and travels on a swiss train.
4. On the trams in Milan
Baltimore has a fun ride on a tram down the centre of the main streets!
5. In Florence
Baltimore puts his finger in the nose of a statue of a Boar!
BONUS Baltimore and the Hats
Baltimore decides he needs a hat and goes off to the hat shop!

Each short story lasts between 3 - 5 minutes
WRITTEN BY: Chris Swingler
VOICE: Chris Swingler

Baltimore and the pop-up radio station - CD. 6 stories.

Baltimore becomes a Radio DJ with his own pop-up radio station.

In his “Sunshine suitcase” there is now a pop-up radio station that can be used to broadcast from wherever he is. So Baltimore entertains the children in the stories as well as the stories being audio entertainment for the listeners to this album 😊 It's quite an adventure.

Tracks
1. The Quizz at the Market Square
His first broadcast is in the centre of town.
2. At the Station
Can you hear Baltimore above the noise of the trains?
3. At the Library
Normally a quiet place but Baltimore gets permission to broadcast from the town library!
4. On the Bus
What a thought, Baltimore broadcasts from a double-decker bus!
5. Next to the Post-box
Always a good crowd at the post-box at Christmas time. Baltimore imagines himself squeezing into an envelope and being whizzed off to an imaginary destination.
6. On Gold Hill
Baltimore meets people who have been to an antiques fair.

Each short story lasts between 3 - 5 minutes
WRITTEN BY: Chris Swingler
VOICE: Chris Swingler

Baltimore at the Lighthouse Hotel - CD. 6 stories.

Baltimore on holiday

In this 3rd album, Baltimore has fun on holiday. He goes on a hike up a mountain with splendid views, he dreams of learning to sail, he tries his hand at painting, stumbles upon a bit of outdoor theatre and gets involved with a chess match on the pier! What a holiday.

Tracks
1. Welcome to the Lighthouse Hotel
Baltimore discovers his holiday hotel and settles in admiring his surroundings.
2. To the top!
Baltimore goes on a hike and has great fun.
3. Ahoy there!
It's a bit windy and Baltimore sees something splashing out at sea.
4. A splash of colour
Baltimore loves painting. What could go wrong?
5. Drama
Off for a walk in the local park, Baltimore hears some sounds and tries to find out what is going on.
6. Board Games on Admiral's day
There is chess match on the pier.

Each short story lasts between 3 - 5 minutes
WRITTEN BY: Chris Swingler
VOICE: Chris Swingler
